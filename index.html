Most Popular

Staffed Bowl Buffet

Our crew sets and serves a streamlined bowl line. Fast throughput, warm hospitality, and clean presentation.

  • Best for 60–300 guests
  • 2–3 staff + lead
  • Setup/teardown included

Drop-Off Buffet

We deliver hot proteins, rice, toppings, and sauces in labeled pans with serving utensils. You serve.

  • Best for 25–150 guests
  • Disposable chafers available
  • Zero-contact option

Individually sealed bowls, clearly labeled for easy distribution and dietary needs.

  • Best for corporate trainings

  • Bulk beverage add-ons

FAQ

Good to know

How far do you travel?

We’re based in the Des Moines metro and travel statewide. A modest travel fee may apply outside the metro.

Can you handle dietary restrictions?

Yes. We regularly support gluten-free, dairy-free, and mild spice preferences. Share counts in your quote request.

Do you provide beverages?

Yes—house lemonade and mango iced tea by the gallon. Cups, ice, and dispensers on request.

What about staffing and setup?

Staffed buffets include our crew, setup, line service, and teardown. Drop-off includes labeled pans and utensils.
