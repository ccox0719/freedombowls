Our Story

Veteran owned. Community rooted.

Freedom Bowls began in a family kitchen with a single rice base and a world of sauces. Today we roll across bases, markets, and block parties with bowls that celebrate Caribbean spice, Thai heat, smoky BBQ, and Mediterranean zest.

The Freedom Roots Foundation reinvests proceeds into mentorship nights, leadership labs, and food security programs for military-connected youth. When you dine with us, you extend the table to someone else.

  • One-line kitchen flow with handcrafted sauces
  • Premium trailer experience with light + dark brand modes
  • Mission-driven events supporting Freedom Roots

Freedom Roots Foundation

Impact with every bowl

We go beyond tasty bowls. Every event funds youth leadership, community meals, and mentorship for military-connected families.

Why helping military youth matters

Military kids shoulder deployment, moves, and sacrifice long before adulthood. Investing in their strength, confidence, and belonging keeps the whole force resilient.

How Freedom Roots shows up:

Built belonging

Guard-specific events where kids connect with peers who understand their story.

Developed resilience

Leadership intensives, camps, and mentoring turn constant change into confidence.

Strengthened families

Parents and caregivers get resources and community to keep homes mission-ready.

20+ years investing in youth—13 focused on military-connected kids—means Chris has already launched mentorship pipelines, mobile camps, and on-base pop-ups. Now he’s scaling that momentum to open more opportunities for every military kid we meet.
